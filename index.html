Evolution of HTML

  • In 1989 Tim Berners-Lee invents the web and HTML.
  • In 1993 Mosaic is released.
  • In 1994 HTML2 is released.
  • In late 1994 the World Wide Web Consortium forms.
  • In 1995 many new tags are added.
  • In 1995 Internet Explorer is released.
  • In late 1995 style sheets for HTML documents begin to take shape.
  • In 1997 HTML3.2 is released
  • In 2014 HTML5 is released
  • New form features and semantic are added.
  • HTML5 allowed for better graphic and 3D on web pages.
